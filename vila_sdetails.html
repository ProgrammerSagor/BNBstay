About This Villa

Perched on the iconic cliffs of Santorini, Azure Cliffside Retreat offers breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea from its multiple terraces and private infinity pool. This modern 4-bedroom villa combines traditional Cycladic architecture with contemporary luxury.

The villa spans two levels, with the main living area featuring an open-plan design that seamlessly connects the lounge, dining area, and fully equipped kitchen. Floor-to-ceiling windows flood the space with natural light and frame the spectacular sea views. Each of the four en-suite bedrooms is designed for comfort and privacy, with the master suite boasting its own private terrace.
